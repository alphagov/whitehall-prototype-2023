HM Land Registry: Sign your mortgage deed

Our digital mortgage service allows homeowners who are remortgaging to sign their mortgage deed quickly and securely.

From:
HM Land Registry
Published
28 January 2019
Last updated
21 June 2021 — See all updates

Applies to England and Wales

Details

Our services are now running again as normal.

You may not be able to rely on search results or our information services if you used them between 1pm and 3pm on Friday 25 November. More information will be available as soon as possible.

Sign your mortgage deed is HM Land Registry’s digital mortgage service.

Lenders and conveyancers can use the service to create and register legal mortgages of residential property for homeowners who are remortgaging.

Homeowners who are remortgaging can use the service to sign their mortgage deed quickly and securely.
